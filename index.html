Cats are escape artists, especially when they don't want to wear something. They can wriggle their way out, bend their way out or shred their way out. Since it's their instinct to lick their wounds, you can't keep a bandage on a cat, even with another bandage. When they're injured or diseased, this instinct can cause infection and delay healing. That's why we invented The Cat's PajamasTM protective body suit for cats with skin problems and post-operative needs.
